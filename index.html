About

Hey there! I'm Gabriela Araujo, a 35-year-old with an unbreakable passion for Art. Currently, I'm studying to migrate to a field that truly speaks to my heart. I'm an unabashed geek, deeply into comic books, manga, anime, and films with captivating storylines and unique aesthetics — Tim Burton is one my all-time favorite! When it comes to series, I'm hooked on "Legion," "Six Feet Under," and "The End of the F... World." And let's not forget about my love for creating captivating collages. Let's embark on a creative journey together!✨
